About Me

15+ years building software, leading teams, and shipping quality work

The Story

I started as a customer service representative. Twenty years in retail, support, and customer-facing roles taught me something that still drives everything I do: listen, understand, and solve the right problem.

When I transitioned to development, those skills stuck with me. I learned to code because I wanted to build things that actually worked. For 15+ years, I've done exactly that — built production applications, mentored developers, and shipped on schedule.

I don't code in isolation. I lead teams. I mentor junior developers. I translate between business needs and technical reality. And I take pride in the work — every line of code, every architecture decision, every deployment.

If you're looking for someone who shows up, delivers quality, and actually cares about the craft, that's me.
